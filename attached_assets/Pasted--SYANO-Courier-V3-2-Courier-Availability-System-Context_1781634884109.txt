# BUSINESS GOAL

Couriers must be able to explicitly choose whether they are available to receive delivery missions.

The system must know:

```text
Can this courier receive a mission right now?
```

Future assignment logic will rely entirely on this answer.

---

# COURIER STATES

Create a permanent courier availability state machine.

Statuses:

```ts
OFFLINE

ONLINE

BUSY
```

---

# STATUS DEFINITIONS

## OFFLINE

Courier is not accepting delivery missions.

Cannot receive assignments.

Ignored by future assignment engine.

---

## ONLINE

Courier is actively available.

Can receive delivery missions.

Can participate in future assignment selection.

---

## BUSY

Courier currently has an active mission.

Cannot receive additional missions.

Future assignment engine must ignore BUSY couriers.

---

# BUSINESS RULE

One courier can only handle:

```text
1 active mission
```

at a time.

Never allow:

```text
Courier
↓
Mission A

Mission B
```

simultaneously.

This is a hard business rule for launch.
