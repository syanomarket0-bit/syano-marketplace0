# DELIVERY ASSIGNMENT MODEL

This is the official SYANO assignment strategy.

---

## STEP 1

Mission created.

Status:

```text
PENDING
```

---

## STEP 2

Find available couriers.

Criteria:

Courier must be:

```text
ONLINE
Accepting Deliveries = true
No Active Mission
Approved
Active
```

---

## STEP 3

Calculate nearest couriers.

Use courier location.

Use pickup location.

Sort by distance ascending.

---

## STEP 4

Take:

```text
Nearest 3 Couriers
```

Only.

Not 5.

Not all.

Exactly 3.

---

## STEP 5

Send mission offer.

Status:

```text
OFFERED
```

for all 3.

---

## STEP 6

Start countdown.

```text
60 seconds
```

Mission offer expires automatically.

---

## STEP 7

First courier who accepts:

Wins mission.

Immediately:

```text
Mission
→ ASSIGNED

Courier
→ BUSY
```

---

## STEP 8

Automatically cancel offers for remaining couriers.

Status:

```text
EXPIRED
```

---

# TIMEOUT LOGIC

If nobody accepts.

---

Round 1:

Nearest 3

Wait:

```text
60 sec
```

---

Round 2:

Next nearest 3

Wait:

```text
60 sec
```

---

Round 3:

Next nearest 3

Wait:

```text
60 sec
```

---

After 3 rounds:

Status:

```text
NO_COURIER_FOUND
```

---

Create:

```text
Dispatch Alert
```

for admin.

# ACCEPT FLOW

Courier presses:

```text
Accept
```

---

System:

Locks mission.

Assigns courier.

Changes status.

Marks BUSY.

Cancels all other offers.

---

Must be atomic.

Prevent race conditions.

If two couriers press accept simultaneously:

Only one wins.

---

# DECLINE FLOW

Courier presses:

```text
Decline
```

---

Status:

```text
DECLINED
```

---

System continues waiting for remaining couriers.
