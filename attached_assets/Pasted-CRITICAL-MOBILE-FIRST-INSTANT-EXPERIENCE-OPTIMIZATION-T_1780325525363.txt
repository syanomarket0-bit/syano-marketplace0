CRITICAL MOBILE-FIRST INSTANT EXPERIENCE OPTIMIZATION

This task is specifically focused on MOBILE DEVICES.

Mobile experience is the highest priority.

Desktop optimization is secondary.

The marketplace must feel like a modern native mobile application rather than a traditional website.

══════════════════════════════════════
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
══════════════════════════════════════

When a mobile user taps any button, card, link, menu item, notification, category, product, store, cart icon, checkout button, or dashboard item:

The destination should appear immediately or almost immediately.

Users should never feel that they are waiting.

The experience should feel comparable to:

* TikTok
* Instagram
* Amazon App
* Temu
* AliExpress App
