MOBILE PRODUCT CARD REQUIREMENTS

IMAGE AREA

* Product image must become the dominant element.
* Increase image height significantly.
* Image should occupy roughly 60% of the card.
* Maintain consistent aspect ratio.
* Use object-fit correctly.
* Image must be larger than all text areas combined visually.

TITLE AREA

* Maximum 2 lines.
* Use line-clamp.
* Never allow title overflow.
* Never allow title to push other content.
* Same reserved height in Arabic and English.

SELLER AREA

* Fixed-height section.
* Seller name must stay on one line.
* Truncate with ellipsis when needed.
* No wrapping.
* No layout shifting.

RATING AREA

* Fixed-height section.
* Stars and review text must align correctly.
* No wrapping.
* No overlap.
* Same layout in both languages.

PRICE AREA

* Price must be visually stronger than seller and rating.
* Discount and old price must align properly.
* Prevent overflow on small devices.

CARD HEIGHT

* Every card on mobile must have identical height.
* No card may become taller because of language differences.
* No card may become taller because of long titles.
