## PHASE 1 — GOAL

Implement a premium mobile sticky purchase bar.

Inspiration:

* Amazon
* Noon
* Trendyol
* AliExpress

Behavior:

While user scrolls below the primary purchase section:

Show a sticky bottom purchase bar.

While user is viewing the top purchase section:

Hide the sticky bar.

No duplication on screen.

Only one purchase experience visible at a time.

## PHASE 6 — VISIBILITY RULES

Use IntersectionObserver.

Observe the existing purchase card.

Rules:

Purchase card visible:

Sticky bar hidden.

Purchase card leaves viewport:

Sticky bar appears.

Purchase card returns:

Sticky bar disappears.

No scroll event spam.

No polling.

No performance hacks.
