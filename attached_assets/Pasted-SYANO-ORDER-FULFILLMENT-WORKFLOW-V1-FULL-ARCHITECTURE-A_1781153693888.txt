ROLE RESPONSIBILITIES
=====================

CUSTOMER

Can:

* View status
* Cancel order before courier pickup

Cannot:

* Change workflow statuses

---

SELLER

Can:

Pending → Confirmed

Pending → CancelledBySeller

Confirmed → Preparing

Preparing → ReadyForPickup

Cannot modify the order after courier pickup.

---

COURIER

Can:

CourierAssigned → PickedUp

PickedUp → OutForDelivery

OutForDelivery → Delivered

OutForDelivery → DeliveryFailed

---

ADMIN

Can:

* View all orders
* Override statuses if necessary
* Assign couriers
* Cancel orders
* Resolve delivery failures

==================================================
CUSTOMER CANCELLATION POLICY
============================

This is mandatory.

Customer cancellation is allowed until:

ReadyForPickup

Meaning:

Pending
Confirmed
Preparing
ReadyForPickup

→ Customer may cancel.

Once status becomes:

PickedUp

Customer can NO LONGER cancel directly.

Reason:

Courier has already received the package.

Future cancellation-request workflows can be added later.
