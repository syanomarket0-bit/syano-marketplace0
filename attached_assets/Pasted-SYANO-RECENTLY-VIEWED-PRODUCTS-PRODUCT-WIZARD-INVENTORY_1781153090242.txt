==================================

CURRENT PROBLEM

Current flow appears to be:

Step 2

Pricing

* Price
* Stock

Step 3

Variants

User may later enable variants.

When variants are enabled:

Inventory becomes controlled by variant stock.

Example:

Product stock = 50

Then:

Red = 10
Blue = 20
Black = 20

The original stock field becomes confusing because it no longer represents actual inventory.
