📋 Phase 10 — Seller Review System Audit
Q: Can customers create store reviews?
✅ Yes. POST /api/sellers/:id/reviews accepts communicationRating, shippingRating, professionalismRating, and optional comment. Requires customer role + active account.

Q: Only after delivery?
✅ Yes — enforced server-side. The route checks for an existing order with status = 'delivered' between the customer and that seller before allowing submission.

Q: Is there a submission UI?
⚠️ Partial. The store page (/store/[slug].tsx) has a ReviewsTab that displays reviews and a summary. However, there is no customer-facing form to actually submit a review. The usePostSellerReview hook exists in the API client but is not wired to any visible UI component.

Q: Is store rating separate from product rating?
✅ Yes. Completely separate tables and endpoints:

Store: seller_reviews table → GET /api/sellers/:id/reviews
Product: reviews table → separate reviews.ts route
Q: Does trust score use sellerReviewsTable?
✅ Yes — accounts for 35 points out of 100:

Store Rating: up to 25 pts (weighted avg of communication 40%, shipping 30%, professionalism 30%)
Review Count: up to 10 pts (log-scale based on number of reviews)
