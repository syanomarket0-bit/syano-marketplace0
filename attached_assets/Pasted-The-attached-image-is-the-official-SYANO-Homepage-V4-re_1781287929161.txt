The attached image is the official SYANO Homepage V4 reference.

This is not inspiration.

This is the target homepage architecture and visual direction.

Implement this homepage as closely as possible.

Project:
SYANO (سوق سوريا)

Type:
Premium Multi-Vendor Marketplace

--------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
--------------------------------------------------

Do not redesign the homepage.

Do not create alternative concepts.

Do not reinterpret the layout.

Follow the attached reference as the primary specification.

Use existing SYANO design tokens, components, and systems where possible.

--------------------------------------------------
CORE PHILOSOPHY
--------------------------------------------------

The homepage must feel like a marketplace immediately.

Users should understand within 3 seconds:

- Products are available
- Categories are available
- Trusted sellers exist
- Shopping can begin immediately

The homepage should feel closer to a premium marketplace than a landing page.

--------------------------------------------------
ABOVE THE FOLD
--------------------------------------------------

Implement the exact structure shown in the reference:

LEFT SIDE:

- SYANO value proposition
- Headline
- Supporting copy
- Search-focused experience
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA

RIGHT SIDE:

- Product Mosaic
- 4 real marketplace products
- Product image
- Product title
- Product price
- Discount badge when available

Products must be visible immediately.

The hero must sell products, not branding.

--------------------------------------------------
HERO RULES
--------------------------------------------------

NO:

- giant hero image
- stock photography
- lifestyle photography
- dashboard-style category tiles
- empty marketing space

YES:

- products
- prices
- commerce signals
- trust signals

Keep hero compact.

Avoid 75vh / 80vh / 90vh hero heights.

Target a premium compact layout.

--------------------------------------------------
SEARCH
--------------------------------------------------

Search is a primary discovery tool.

Make search highly visible.

Search should feel central to the homepage experience.

--------------------------------------------------
CATEGORIES
--------------------------------------------------

Below the hero:

Popular Categories

Use category cards.

Do NOT use:

- pill chips
- oversized colored squares
- dashboard styling

Categories should feel premium and marketplace-oriented.

--------------------------------------------------
SECTION ORDER
--------------------------------------------------

Implement this order:

1. Header
2. Hero + Product Mosaic
3. Popular Categories
4. Today's Deals
5. Best Sellers
6. Verified Stores
7. New Arrivals
8. Explore Categories
9. Seller + Courier CTA Band
10. Footer

--------------------------------------------------
VERIFIED STORES
--------------------------------------------------

Leverage the existing Trust System.

Show verified sellers prominently.

Use:

- trust score
- verification status
- seller credibility

Trust should be visible on the homepage.

--------------------------------------------------
CONTENT INDEPENDENCE
--------------------------------------------------

Homepage must look complete even when:

- zero hero banners exist
- zero deals exist
- zero admin banners exist

Do not rely on admin-created content to make the homepage look good.

--------------------------------------------------
MOBILE
--------------------------------------------------

Mobile experience is critical.

Requirements:

- products visible quickly
- minimal scrolling before commerce content
- premium RTL experience
- native Arabic feel

Do not create a full-screen mobile hero.

--------------------------------------------------
VISUAL DIRECTION
--------------------------------------------------

Target:

- Apple-level cleanliness
- Noon-level commerce density
- Trendyol-level browsing
- Modern premium ecommerce

Avoid:

- SaaS landing page aesthetics
- dashboard aesthetics
- excessive empty space
- decorative layouts

--------------------------------------------------
CLEANUP
--------------------------------------------------

Remove obsolete homepage structures.

Remove old fallback-only logic if replaced.

Remove duplicate sections.

Keep architecture clean.
