==================================================
CORRECT ARCHITECTURE
====================

The timer must be based on:

startDate
endDate

stored in the database.

Example:

Deal:

* starts: 2025-09-01 00:00 UTC
* ends: 2025-09-07 23:59 UTC

The timer should always calculate:

remainingTime = endDate - currentServerTime

NOT:

remainingTime = loginTime
remainingTime = userSessionTime
remainingTime = browserStorageTime
