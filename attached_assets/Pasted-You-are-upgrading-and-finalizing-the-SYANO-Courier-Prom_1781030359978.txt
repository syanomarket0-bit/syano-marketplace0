# BUSINESS RULE

A user can only have ONE active role.

Allowed roles:

* customer
* seller
* courier
* admin

A user MUST NOT be:

* customer + courier
* customer + seller
* seller + courier

at the same time.

The role must be exclusive.

Workflow:

Customer → applies → approved → becomes Courier

Customer → applies → approved → becomes Seller

Role transition must be clear and consistent across the entire system.
