## GOAL

At checkout:

The customer must explicitly choose their delivery zone from the existing delivery zones configured in the system.

After selecting the zone:

The customer must enter the exact delivery address/details beneath it.

This creates a structured delivery flow:

1. Delivery Zone
2. Detailed Address
3. Checkout

---

## CURRENT PROBLEM

The checkout currently allows entering address information without a strong connection to delivery zones.

This causes:

* inconsistent addresses
* delivery confusion
* courier assignment issues
* incorrect delivery pricing
