You are upgrading the entire **SYANO (سوق سوريا)** product variant system.

## Goal

Replace the current basic variant implementation with a modern marketplace-grade system similar to Shopify, Amazon, Trendyol, and Noon.

This is an enhancement of the existing system, NOT a rewrite of the marketplace.

---

# CRITICAL RULES

* Do NOT break existing products.
* Do NOT remove existing database columns unless absolutely necessary.
* Prefer additive migrations.
* Preserve all existing APIs whenever possible.
* Keep backward compatibility with products that have no variants.
* Preserve RTL support.
* Preserve Arabic and English translations.
* Preserve current theme, colors, spacing, shadows, and border radius.
* No placeholder UI.
* No ugly admin-style forms.
* TypeScript must finish with 0 errors.
* Mobile app compatibility must remain intact.
